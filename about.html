About Us

“Flavors is like the virtue of India,
Namakwala puts a crown to that virtue..”

The Indian salt manufacturing and export industry has become almost synonymous with the Namakwala brand. Redefining the salt industry in India since 1945, Namakwala was established by Late Shri Badri Lal Lohia. Late Shri Lohia was a pioneer in modern approaches. His vision was for Namakwala to reach global prominence through quality and innovation.

After 75 years, Namakwala remains a leading global salt manufacturer, with clients worldwide. At Namakwala, we craft more than just salt; we create flavor. Imagine a world without salt. How tasteless it would be! Our high-quality salt meets the world’s nutritional iodine requirements.

We also produce salt for industrial applications, catering to both the food and nutrition industries. Salt may not be poetic, but it's essential. Food isn’t truly food without it, and numerous industrial applications depend on it.

Whatever the demand is, Namakwala has it covered. Our 75+ years of expertise drive us to expand further. Namakwala’s mission is to reach the hearts of people, adding flavor to their food and lives, and alleviating the mundane. We manufacture fine salt crystals with a vision to ease worldly tensions and invigorate your meals and lives.
