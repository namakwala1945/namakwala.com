Largest Salt Exporter from India to Dubai

Founded by Late Shri Badri Lal Lohia, Namakwala is a force to be reckoned with in the salt industry. We are a leading Indian salt manufacturing company that specializes in manufacturing and exporting industrial, refined, tablet, iodized, table, rock and raw salt. Fulfilling the diverse salt requirements of our myriad customers across the globe, we enjoy an unmatched global presence.

We are the largest salt exporter in India, serving our esteemed clientele with a difference for the last 73 years. We have a come a long way since our inceptions and are exporting salt to more than 50 countries today. They include Algeria, Egypt, Lesotho, Angola, Equatorial Guinea, Liberia, Eritrea, Libya, Botswana, Ethiopia, Marshall Islands, Burkina Faso, Fiji, Micronesia, and Federated States of Nauru, Burundi, Gabon, Palau, and Cameroon. The Gambia, Republic of Congo, Cape Verde, Ghana, Samoa, Central African Republic, Guinea, Solomon Islands, Chad, Guinea Bissau and Tonga are the other countries where we export our salt. In addition, we also do the salt export from India to Dubai, Abu Dhabi and other Middle East countries like Saudi Arabia, Bahrain.

We at Namakwala strive to produce high-quality salt using state-of-the-art equipment and technologies. Our quality management team goes overboard for ensuring purity and long shelf life of the salt produced. Salt leaving our production unit speaks volumes of purity, nutrition and the right amount of iodine. The salt that we export across the globe is also preservative free. Besides making our salt production as high in quality as possible, we take the required measures to make salt affordable for our customers as well. In all, we strive to provide the utmost satisfaction to every customer. Not surprisingly, we manage to add new and more customers to our ever-growing network every year.

With our fully automatic salt manufacturing process, we produce salt in environmentally friendly conditions and pack it hygienically. It is a matter of sheer pride for us that our salt boasts the highest purity of up to 99.50% sodium chloride content. In addition, it contains the right iodine level specified by the FSSAI (of the government of India) and the WHO. A number of other reasons, such as ISO 9001:2015 certification, also make Namakwala a cut above the rest. We have received several prestigious certifications, including HACCP, HALAL and FSSAI, which all are the testimony to our excellence in the field.

At Namakwala, we have earned a formidable reputation for being one of the top industrial salt manufacturers in India. Not just in India but our industrial salt is in great demand all across the globe as well. We are renowned as a leading salt exporter in Dubai that offers a range of industrial salt, including pure salt used in fields related to textile dyeing, pharma, speciality chemical, water softening and UV fluid industry. The crystalline salt that we offer is used in industries like distemper, drilling salt, paint, chemicals, sugar, dyes, etc. We also offer medium grade salt related to dyes, detergent, chemicals, and sea & foreign food industry. Other industry salts that we offer include superfine salt, coarse salt, PVD salt, 30 mesh salt and de-icing salt.
