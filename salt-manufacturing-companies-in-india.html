#1 SALT MANUFACTURING COMPANIES IN INDIA

Founded by the Late Shri Badri Lal Lohia, the Namakwala Group has grown since its inception many years ago into one of the most recognizable, reputed and trustworthy salt manufacturing companies in India. We are the leading manufacturer and exporter of iodized/industrialized salt, as well as a number of spices.

With the descendants of Late Shri Badri Lal Lohia helming us, we create an unfazed commitment and endeavour to prompt existing and new services with each passing day. Our dedication to attaining 100% customer satisfaction with each of our products is what makes us a market leader in terms of adding as well as retaining clients from diversified sectors of the community. The progress we have attained in these years has also prompted us to provide our customers with maximum value-added services, with our moral values and ethical code of conduct guiding us on a day to day basis to become the largest producer of salt in India.

As one of the top 10 salt companies in India, we engage in the manufacture and export of quality refined, iodized, industrialized, rock, table, tablet and raw salt. We make use of state of the art technology, equipment and production methods to achieve excellence and quality in the field.

Our salt manufacturing process is fully automatic, thus ensuring that the salt we produce and pack adheres to the strictest hygiene and environmentally friendly conditions. We take pride in revealing that Namakwala’s salt has the highest purity of up to 99.50% sodium chloride (NaCl) content while containing the right levels of iodine as per the norms set by the FSSAI (of the Government of India) and the WHO.

Is your salt pure enough? This is the question we ask our customers as we continually work to provide healthy, safe salt and spices, which have passed several rigorous quality tests, to households around India. As a reputable salt company in India, we back our claims with numerous certifications that work as accreditation to our work. Accordingly, we are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and we have earned several other prestigious certifications, include HACCP, HALAL and FSSAI. In addition to ensuring the purity and long shelf life of the salt and spices that we produce, our quality management team ensures that each grain of salt that leaves our production unit is pure, nutritious and has just the right amount of iodine for proper mental development.

Our brand continues to grow, with our team continually on the search for future markets as well as their changing needs. We attempt to innovatively address these needs by offering a wide range of salt products that cater to different segments of customers, offering them a plethora of choices when it comes to choosing Namakwala salt for their cooking needs. And we do all this while still keeping the prices of our products to the basic minimum and affordable (check our salt export price India) by the quintessential Indian household. Thus, it can be said that in doing so, Namakwala is here to stay and become a game changer in the salt business in India.
