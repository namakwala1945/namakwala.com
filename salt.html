Crystal Salt

Natural Crystal Salt is the most basic condiment as well as a staple food. This salt is obtained from the evaporation of the ocean's water, simply and without artifice.

No synthetic mineral supplement can equal the wealth of minerals that natural Salt supplies, regardless of how rich its content or how precisely it is formulated.  Raw/Common Salt is used in Chloro Alkali Plants, Chlorine Plant, Paper Industry, Deicing, Lather Industry, Pickles, Ayurvedic Preparations, Water Softening and many more industries.
